About Us

K L Deemed to be University has a spacious canteen with the latest equipment and a hygienic environment, providing quality food and prompt service that caters to the needs of all students and staff.

The central cafeteria of 1500 Sq.m. is available on campus, along with mini cafes and fast-food centers in various blocks. The canteen is open from 6:30 a.m. to 8:30 p.m., offering a wide variety of North-Indian and South-Indian cuisine. Cool aqua water is also available.

This website, created by students, aims to enhance canteen services by offering a platform where users can explore menus, check prices, and place orders from various cafeterias. Each cafeteria has its own cart system, meaning orders can only be placed from one cafeteria at a time, ensuring smooth and efficient service.
